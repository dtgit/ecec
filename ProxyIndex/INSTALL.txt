Installing ProxyIndex

  Installing ProxyIndex is easy; it is just a standard Zope products
  installation. Drop ProxyIndex into the Products directory of your
  Zope, restart Zope and you should be done.

  ProxyIndex can be downloaded from:

    http://www.zope.org/Members/infrae/ProxyIndex
